Meet The Crew

Xavier Gallo

Podcast Host

Xavier is a current senior captain of the Hilliard Davidson Cross Country team - one of the most dominant squads in Ohio. Xavier, who is a varsity returner from the 2018 D1 state runner-up team, has been able to aquire a 4:40 1600 and a 10:00.22 3200; both of which were run indoors. Xavier also holds a PR of 16:50 in a 5k. Xavier considers himself a long(er) distance runner who excels in events from the 5k up; nevertheless, he has managed to run a 55 second 400. While not running, Xavier will spend his time reading, biking, or simply spending time with his running friends.



Nick DeSilva

Podcast Host

Nick DeSilva is currently a senior captain of the Hilliard Davidson Cross country team. Though not a varsity runner, Nick is a part of the very deep JV squad that is looking to show the depth of the whole team and win some races. Nick owns PRs of 17:50 in the 5K and has shown his speed in the 400 with a 55 second relay split that got his squad 2nd in the JV league meet 4x4.

Brian Stroh

Sound Engineer

Brian Stroh is a senior of the Hilliard Davidson Cross Country team. He has always been either at the back of the varsity pack or front of the JV pack, but he has a goal to be a part of the varsity team at states this upcoming season. His 5k is a 17:09, but he is hoping to bring that down this year after suffering from a back injury last year. He isn't much of a short to middle distance guy, but anything 5k and above he loves.

Mitchell Montgomery

Producer

Mitch “Monty” Montgomery is currently a senior captain for Hilliard Davidson cross country. Mitch, who is a varsity returner from both the 2017 and 2018 D-1 state runner up teams, has ran a 2:04 800, a 4:38 1600, and a 10:02 2 mile. In cross country, he holds a 16:43 PR in the 5k. In his free time, Mitch loves watching movies, playing video games, and hanging out with his teammates.





Nate Gilbert

Social Media Manager

Nate Gilbert is currently a junior captain of the Hilliard Davidson cross country team. Nate got his first taste of varsity racing sporadically during his sophomore year, and he is a part of the varsity squad now. He is more of an anaerobic runner and prefers racing on the track, but he still loves cross country. He has PRs of 4:40 in the 1600m, 10:01 in the 3200m, and 16:58 in the 5k
