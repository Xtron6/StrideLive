Running Through the Mind

The Running Through the Mind Podcast, hosted by Xavier Gallo and Nick Desilva delves into what thoughts come across the mind while running. Our aim for this podcast is to further spread the love for running in a cool and fun manner.Each Wednesday we premiere an all new podcast episode; many of which feature a very special guest. Both Xavier and Nick are current runners and captains on the Hilliard Davidson Cross Country program.
